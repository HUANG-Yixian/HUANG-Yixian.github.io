About Me

Hello! I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Learning Disabilities and Behavioral Disorders(LD/BD) at the University of Texas at Austin (UT Austin). Before that, I studied M.Ed. in Educational Psychology at McGill Univeristy. I am a quantitative researcher, and am interested in randomized controlled trial (RCT) and meta-analysis research.

Research Interests

My research interests center around language and literacy development in children, with attention to English learners and students with special needs. Specifically, I am focusing on developing evidence-based interventions and assessments for children with dyslexia and/or dyscalculia.

I am currently looking for research opportunities broadly related to language development and cognitive neuroscience.
